Here are 10 Helpful Tips

  1. Take the word CAN'T out your vocabulary
  2. Always ask questions (your instructors and student leadership are there to help you. don't be afraid to ask for help
  3. Try your best
  4. Never doubt your capabilities
  5. GO HOME AND PRACTICE
  6. Always be on time!! (in the band world, to be on time, is to be EARLY)
  7. Practice at a slower tempo than the regular tempo
  8. Don't be afraid to BREAK DOWN YOUR MUSIC (learning your music is not a race, as long as your music is learned, and you are trying to learn, your instructor will be pleased)
  9. Be CONFIDENT in yourself
  10. Last but not least, always have fun!! If this is your passion you should do it with some passion
